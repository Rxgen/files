Board's Report

The world of energy is changing fast. Geopolitical factors, regulatory and policy changes, price fluctuations, to name a few, continue to exert new and complex influences on the industry.

As a new-age integrated downstream Company, Nayara Energy has made progress in realizing the potential - in the market, business, infrastructure and most importantly, for its people. In summary, the financial year 2018-19 was a pivotal year for the Company.
